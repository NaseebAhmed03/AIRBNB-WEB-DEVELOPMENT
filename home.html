Hosting Tips

Whether you are thinking of starting to list your vacation rental property on Airbnb or you are an expert Airbnb host, my tips will help you make the most of this amazing rentals website to achieve your goals.

STR Hosting Life Cycle

Research & Setup

  • Do vacation rental market research for your area on AirDNA
  • Identify the right price for your property
  • Hire an Airbnb property management company if needed.
  • Prepare Airbnb cleaning checklist
  • Furnish it with Airbnb essentials
  • Ensure it meets Airbnb Trust & Safety standards
  • Take pictures of the property
  • Identify the 'Key' handover process
  • Create your property listing
  • Setup Availability calendar and pricing

Running the show

  • Respond to guest queries automatically with smartbnb
  • Create guidebook
  • Send check-in instructions with house rules
  • Share emergency services numbers
  • Share local tips, restaurant etc
  • Prepare the property for guest arrival
  • Ensure guest is checked-in
  • Inform the guest about the check-out procedure
  • Review guests

Operations & Maintenance

  • Ensure Amenities Listed On Airbnb are available for guest and stocked up
  • Ensure cleaning is done after the guest departs
  • Look for any damages by the previous guest
  • Ensure property, and laundry cleaning meets the standard you promise to guests
